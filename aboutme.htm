About Me

Max Simpson is a recent graduate from Carnegie Mellon University with a Bachelor of Science degree. He is interested in almost any type of technical challenge, whether it be mechanical, electrical, virtual, or theoretical.

In his spare time he enjoys hiking, reading, coding, knitting, and golf among other activities.

Max pushing a 'buggy' across the finish line during Carnegie Mellon's annual competition.

Note: Buggies are high-tech, composite racing vehicles akin to soapbox cars built by students in their free time. They reach speeds of up to 40 mph powered by gravity alone and are pushed around in a relay race type event. (Yes, there is a person inside there)
